## Top-3 findings (single sentence each)

1. **A100 wall has `PhysicsArticulationRootAPI` + `PhysicsCollisionAPI` but no `PhysicsRigidBodyAPI` or explicit FixedBase flag** — PhysX articulations typically require the root link to be either a rigid body or a `fixedBase=True` articulation; omission here may leave the wall floating or be silently treated as world-fixed depending on PhysX version. **MEDIUM** — failure_modes #4 covers hinge axis misalignment but not the root-rigidity question; recommended patch is either `apply_api_schema(prim_path=wall, schema_name="PhysicsRigidBodyAPI") + set_attribute("physics:rigidBodyEnabled", value=False)` for a kinematic root, OR an explicit `set_attribute(articulation:fixedBase, value=True)` on the wall. Function-gate run will surface this; for now this is form-gate-clean but a function-gate hazard.

2. **A101 implements VERTICAL insertion but task name is `PegInsertionSide-v0` which is HORIZONTAL side-insertion in ManiSkill v3** — the canonical ships `peg_height` along world +Z with hole opening up, while real ManiSkill PegInsertionSide-v0 has the peg horizontal and the hole opening sideways from a vertical plate. The eval-format dict still mirrors ManiSkill's keys (`success_rate`, `success_at_depth_threshold`, `peg_seated`, `contact_observed`) so A/B comparison against the leaderboard is structurally compatible at the metric layer, but the kinematic shape differs. **MEDIUM honesty** — failure_modes #8 acknowledges this; recommended patch is to expose `peg_insertion_orientation: "vertical"` in role_defaults so retrieval consumers comparing to side-insertion datasets know the substitution boundary.

3. **A93 explicitly demonstrates the OVERLOAD-DIAGNOSE pathway rather than a successful lift** (Franka 3 kg continuous payload < 10 kg cobot-share of 15 kg workpiece) — the canonical is intentionally a diagnostic-shape, with `monitor_joint_effort` capturing the saturation. **LOW** — `roles.primary_robot.constraints` includes `["franka_panda", "ur10", "ur10e"]` so a scenario-profile can swap to UR10 (17 kg payload) to actually complete the lift; the constraints list makes the substitution path explicit. Could be promoted further by adding `payload_realistic_for_lift: false` flag in role_defaults so a query for "successful cobot lift assist" disambiguates from this diagnostic canonical.
