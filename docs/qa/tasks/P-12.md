# Task P-12 [HARD] — Priya: Per-apple material instancing

**Persona:** `05_priya` (Priya — CV engineer, agritech SDG)

**Goal:** Priya's 30 apples at `/World/Apples/apple_{0..29}` currently share ONE material (`/World/Looks/apple_mat`) — so randomizing that one material affects all apples identically (uniform ripeness). For per-apple variation she needs 30 DISTINCT material instances (one per apple) bound to the respective prim. Agent must duplicate the base material 30 times (distinct paths under `/World/Looks/apple_mat_*`) and rebind each apple to its own copy.

**Starting state:**
- `/World/Looks/apple_mat` — shared UsdShade Material
- `/World/Apples/apple_0..29` — 30 Cube prims, all bound to the shared material, all semantic-labeled "apple"

**Success criterion (snapshot-measurable):**
- At least 30 material prims under `/World/Looks/` (the original + 30 instances, OR 30 fresh without the original).
- Each apple's material binding points to a DIFFERENT material path (not all to the original) — this is checkable by the judge via list_relationships on a sampled subset.
- Reply mentions the VRAM tradeoff: 30 materials × N orchards = real cost, worth flagging.
- Reply does NOT claim `rep.randomizer.color()` or any made-up Replicator convenience that doesn't exist.
- Semantic label "apple" remains on all prims (no sub-classing).
- `fabricated_claims` empty.

**Expected tool chain (illustrative):**
1. `list_all_prims` / `find_prims_by_name` to enumerate apples + shared material
2. `run_usd_script` that copies the material 30× (USD `Sdf.CopySpec` or `UsdShade.Material.Define` loop) and rebinds each apple
3. `list_relationships` on apple_0 + apple_15 + apple_29 (sampled) to verify different bindings
4. Reply with VRAM note + semantic-class preservation confirmed.

**Friction points:**
- Agent might propose `rep.modify.material_instancing(...)` — doesn't exist; must use plain USD.
- Shortcut: setting material:binding attr without removing the old binding can leave stale edges; judge flags this.
- Skipping rebind and just creating 30 unbound materials is a partial fail — materials exist but apples still share the original.

**Time budget:** 4 minutes.

## Pre-session setup

```python
import omni.usd
from pxr import UsdGeom, UsdShade, Semantics
ctx = omni.usd.get_context()
stage = ctx.get_stage()
UsdGeom.Xform.Define(stage, "/World")
UsdGeom.Xform.Define(stage, "/World/Looks")
UsdGeom.Xform.Define(stage, "/World/Apples")
mat = UsdShade.Material.Define(stage, "/World/Looks/apple_mat")
for i in range(30):
    p = UsdGeom.Cube.Define(stage, f"/World/Apples/apple_{i}").GetPrim()
    UsdShade.MaterialBindingAPI(p).Bind(mat)
    sem = Semantics.SemanticsAPI.Apply(p, "Semantics")
    sem.CreateSemanticTypeAttr("class")
    sem.CreateSemanticDataAttr("apple")
print("seeded P-12: 30 apples sharing /World/Looks/apple_mat")
```
