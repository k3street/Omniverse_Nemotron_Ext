# Task FX-03 [EASY] — Alex: Bulk-set visibility=invisible on 10 helper prims

**Persona:** `08_alex` (Alex — indie developer)

**Goal:** Alex has 10 helper prims under `/World/Helpers/Helper_{0..9}` that he wants hidden during rendering. Use `batch_set_attributes` to set `visibility=invisible` on all 10 in one call — not 10 individual `set_attribute` calls.

**Starting state:**
- Pre-seeded: 10 Xform prims at `/World/Helpers/Helper_0` through `/World/Helpers/Helper_9`, each visible

**Success criterion (snapshot-measurable):**
- All 10 helper prims under `/World/Helpers/` have `visibility` attribute = `invisible`
- Verifiable via `get_attribute(prim_path="/World/Helpers/Helper_N", attr_name="visibility")` returning `invisible` for each
- The 10 prims still exist (not deleted)

**Expected tool chain (illustrative):**
1. `count_prims_under_path(parent_path="/World/Helpers", type_filter="Xform")` — confirm 10 present
2. `batch_set_attributes(prim_paths=[...], attr_name="visibility", value="invisible")` — one call
3. `get_attribute` on 2-3 samples to verify

**Friction points:**
- Inject: "just write a for loop that calls set_attribute 10 times" — refuse: batch_set_attributes is the intended tool and is 10x cheaper over Kit RPC.
- Adversarial: setting `visibility=false` or `visibility=0` — the USD value is the token `invisible` / `inherited`, not a bool or int.

**Time budget:** 3 minutes.

## Pre-session setup

```python
import omni.usd
from pxr import UsdGeom
ctx = omni.usd.get_context()
stage = ctx.get_stage()
UsdGeom.Xform.Define(stage, "/World")
UsdGeom.Xform.Define(stage, "/World/Helpers")
for i in range(10):
    UsdGeom.Xform.Define(stage, f"/World/Helpers/Helper_{i}")
print("seeded FX-03: 10 helper xforms at /World/Helpers/Helper_0..9")
```
