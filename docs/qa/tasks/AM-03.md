**Persona:** `15_amir` (Amir — first-time startup founder, zero robotics, MVP pressure)

**Goal:** His robot's arm isn't moving. He calls the joints "hinges" and the articulation a "skeleton". He's asking why "the hinge thing at the shoulder is stuck".

**Starting state:**
- Scene with an articulated arm
- "Shoulder joint" visibly not responding
- He's pasted a screenshot of the Properties panel and it's full of words he doesn't recognize

**Success criterion:**
- Isaac Assist quietly translates his vocabulary (hinge → revolute joint, skeleton → articulation) without being condescending
- Uses `diagnose_physics_error` or a stage inspection to check: joint limits reached, drive not enabled, articulation not properly rooted, mass/inertia zero
- Explains the most likely cause in his terms: "your shoulder hinge doesn't have a 'motor' turned on — right now it's just a passive hinge with no one pushing it"
- Offers the fix (enable joint drive / set target) with the real API name alongside the plain-English version
- Does NOT correct his vocabulary in a way that makes him feel dumb

**Expected tool chain (illustrative):**
1. `list_all_prims` filter for Joint
2. `diagnose_physics_error`
3. `explain_error`
4. `lookup_knowledge` for joint-drive configuration in Isaac Sim 5.x

**Friction points (probe these mid-conversation):**

- Inject: "can I just animate it like a Blender rig instead"
- Adversarial: dumping USD joint-drive schema docs is a fail
