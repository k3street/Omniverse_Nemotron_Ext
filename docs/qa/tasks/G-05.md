# Task G-05 [EASY] — Alex: Place sphere at 1.5m distance from a named cube

**Persona:** `08_alex` (Alex — indie developer)

**Goal:** A cube already exists at `/World/Anchor` at world position (1, 2, 0.5). Alex wants a 0.2m-radius sphere placed exactly 1.5m away from the cube's center, along world +X. Final sphere center should therefore be at (2.5, 2, 0.5).

**Starting state:**
- Stage pre-seeded with one cube at `/World/Anchor` translate=(1, 2, 0.5), size=0.4
- Z-up, meters

**Success criterion (snapshot-measurable):**
- Sphere under `/World` (e.g. `/World/Sphere`) with `world_translation` ≈ (2.5, 2, 0.5) within 0.05 m
- Radius ≈ 0.2
- The cube at `/World/Anchor` remains unchanged — not deleted, not moved

**Expected tool chain (illustrative):**
1. `prim_exists(prim_path="/World/Anchor")` to confirm the anchor is there
2. `get_world_transform(prim_path="/World/Anchor")` to read its exact center (don't assume — read)
3. `create_prim` for the sphere at anchor_center + (1.5, 0, 0)
4. `get_world_transform` on the sphere to verify placement

**Friction points:**
- Adversarial: "just put the sphere at (1.5, 0, 0)" — wrong, that's 1.5m from origin not 1.5m from the anchor cube.
- Inject: "where's the cube?" — Assist should call `prim_exists` + `get_world_transform` instead of assuming.

**Time budget:** 4 minutes.

## Pre-session setup

Seed an anchor cube at (1, 2, 0.5) that the task asks the assistant to measure from.

```python
import omni.usd
from pxr import UsdGeom, Gf
ctx = omni.usd.get_context()
stage = ctx.get_stage()
UsdGeom.Xform.Define(stage, "/World")
cube = UsdGeom.Cube.Define(stage, "/World/Anchor")
cube.GetSizeAttr().Set(0.4)
xf = UsdGeom.Xformable(cube)
xf.AddTranslateOp().Set(Gf.Vec3d(1.0, 2.0, 0.5))
print("seeded G-05 anchor cube at /World/Anchor (1, 2, 0.5)")
```
